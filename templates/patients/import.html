Import Rules
Only .csv files are accepted. Excel files must be saved as CSV first.
The first row must be the header row with column names exactly as shown.
Rows with a duplicate matric_no will be skipped — existing records are never overwritten.
New faculties and programmes will be auto-created if they don't already exist.
All imported records are tagged CSV_IMPORT for tracking purposes.
⚠️ Large imports may take a moment. Do not close the browser while importing.
⚠️ Up to 10 row errors are shown after import. Fix and re-upload skipped rows.
